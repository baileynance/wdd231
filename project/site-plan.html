Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is for people who are looking at finding new anime to watch. There will be plenty of suggestions as well as the ability to filter through genres.

Scenarios

What if I have already seen one of the anime on the list?

The idea is that the user can input anime they have already seen and then it will be removed from the list and not appear in the randomly generated anime suggestion.

How will I know if the suggested anime might interest me?

Along with the suggestion of the anime there will include a brief synopsis, the ability to filter out genres you are not interested in, as well as the ability to see what shows it is most similar to.
