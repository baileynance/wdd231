Site-Plan

Site Name: Kard Kol

I chose this name because it combines the words Card and Collection and that is essentially what the function of the webiste will be.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is for people who are interested in all the different types of trading cards. They will be able to sort through and look at all different kinds of trading cards and be abe to add them to a favorites page.

Scenarios

What if I do not know anything about trading cards?

That is not an issue because each of the cards will have information that once you interact with them you are likely to learn more.

How will I be able to save my favorites?

There will be the option to join the website community, so that you can save and share as many cards as you would like!
